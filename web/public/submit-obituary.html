Staff tool

Add an obituary

For a notice that doesn't come through the funeral-home feeds — an out-of-area obituary you've been sent directly. It publishes to the live register in a couple of minutes; no email, no code. Only the name and a date are required.

Access

Your own GitHub fine-grained PAT with Actions: read and write on this repo. It stays in your browser and is never committed. Same token as the Add-a-home page.

The person

A direct link to a portrait image. Leave blank for a name-only card.

The obituary

A one-line summary is composed automatically from the name, age, town, and date of death. The text above becomes the body of the person's page.

This triggers the add-obituary.yml workflow, which appends the entry to data/manual.json, then rebuilds and deploys the site. Watch progress on the Actions tab. To take a published notice back down, remove its entry from manual.json (or add its slug to data/suppressed.json).
